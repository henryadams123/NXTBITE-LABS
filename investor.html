Investment Thesis

WHY NOW.
WHY THIS.

01
Category Timing
The better-for-you mac segment is at the same inflection point protein snacks were in 2018. Early brand leadership in emerging better-for-you categories compounds significantly. Goodles proved it's possible — MELTED's sauce-only format is a more scalable, differentiated entry point.
02
Format Innovation
Every competitor sells full kits. MELTED sells sauce only — pairing with the protein pasta and gluten-free noodles consumers already buy. This isn't a new product category. It's a new entry point into an existing one, with a built-in audience that's already looking for it.
03
Studio Model
NXTBITE LABS is not a single-product bet. The studio model — identify, engineer, brand, scale — is designed to repeat across comfort food categories. MELTED is brand one. The methodology is the asset.
04
Brand Foundation
Most CPG startups underinvest in brand early and pay for it later. NXTBITE LABS is built brand-first — visual identity, voice, and digital presence are in place before launch. This compresses the timeline to earned media and retail readiness.
